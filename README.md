CleanMap is an interactive, user-friendly web platform designed to empower citizens and local authorities to identify, report, and monitor waste in their neighborhoods. It bridges the gap between community action and effective waste management, making environmental sustainability simple, transparent, and actionable.
How It Works:
User-Friendly Reporting:
Citizens can report illegal dumping, litter, or waste hotspots using easy-to-fill forms.
Each report captures title, description, and location to ensure precise tracking.
Real-Time Tracking:
Users can track the status of their reports at a glance:
Pending – Report submitted
Verified – Confirmed by Admin
Cleaned – Waste removed
Interactive maps display all reports, helping users visualize problem areas.
Admin Control & Verification:
Admins can verify, clean, or delete reports to ensure authenticity.
A dedicated Hotspot Map highlights areas with repeated dumping, enabling authorities to prioritize cleanup efficiently.
Community Engagement & Transparency:
By showing the status of reports in real time, CleanMap keeps the community informed and motivated.
Users feel empowered, knowing their efforts directly contribute to a cleaner, safer environment.
Impactful Visualizations:
Color-coded status badges and map markers make it easy to identify urgent areas.
Hotspot maps visualize critical zones, helping citizens and authorities focus on the most polluted locations first.
