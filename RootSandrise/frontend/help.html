Help & Support

CleanMap is a community-driven platform that helps identify and reduce illegal waste dumping. This page guides you on how to use the platform effectively.

How to Use CleanMap

  1. Create an account or login to CleanMap.
  2. Navigate to your user dashboard.
  3. Click on “Report Waste”.
  4. Upload a photo of the waste.
  5. Enter location details accurately.
  6. Submit the report.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can report illegal dumping?

Any registered user can report waste dumping incidents in their neighborhood.

Is my identity visible to others?

No. User information is kept confidential and is visible only to administrators.

How are reports verified?

Reports are reviewed by NGOs or administrators before being marked as verified.

What happens after reporting?

Once verified, the report appears on the map and authorities are notified for cleanup.
